Settings

Appearance

Choose how Tideline looks. System follows your device's light/dark setting; the named palettes pin a look regardless.

Inbox decay

How many days a newly captured link lives in your inbox before it washes out to the Flotsam. Lower it to feel more pressure; raise it for a gentler tide. Applies to links you add from now on.

Wallabag

Links you choose to keep are pushed to Wallabag for reading and archiving. Works with a self-hosted instance or the hosted service (https://app.wallabag.it). Create an API client under Wallabag's Developer settings to get a client ID and secret.
